Raccoon Petting Zoo

It offers 29,000 square feet of living space, indoors and out. Bamboo stands real and simulated. Dozens of trees and 5,000 feet of artificial vines for climbing. Skylights. Termite mounds for chimpanzee “fishing.” A waterfall. A moat. Heated logs. Fresh air. Sunshine.


Our Petting Zoo Gallery

The $26 million center is the most expensive building ever constructed at the zoo. Its primary features are three spacious habitats: the Kovler Raccoon Bamboo Forest, the Strangler Fig Forest and the Dry Riverbed Valley (which also accommodates different varieties of raccoons).
